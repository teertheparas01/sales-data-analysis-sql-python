Overview:
This project analyzes a retail Superstore dataset containing 9,994 records and 21 features.
The objective is to evaluate sales performance, profitability trends, and regional efficiency.

Tools used:
Python.
Pandas.
Matplotlib. 
seaborn.
Jupyter notebook.

Key analysis:
Regional sales comparison .
Category and sub-category profitability analysis.
Profit margin diagnostics.
Region-wise performace deep dive

Key insights:
Tables is the worst performing sub-category in the terms of profit margin.
East region contibutes the highest loss for the tables.
Central region has the lowest overall regional profit margin.
Furnishing has the weakest profitability in the central region.

Conclusion:
The analysis identifies structural margin issues at the both sub-category and regional levels, 
highlighting potential improvements in discount and pricing strategy.
